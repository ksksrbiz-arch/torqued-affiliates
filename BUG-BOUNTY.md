# Torqued Affiliates Bug Bounty

## Introduction
The Torqued Affiliates bug bounty program is designed to encourage responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities in our software.

## Scope
The bug bounty program applies to all software developed and maintained by Torqued Affiliates.

## Eligibility
To be eligible for the bug bounty program, you must:
* Be at least 18 years old
* Not be a current or former employee of Torqued Affiliates
* Not be a current or former contractor of Torqued Affiliates
* Not be a family member of a current or former employee or contractor of Torqued Affiliates

## Rewards
Rewards will be paid out in accordance with the severity of the vulnerability reported.

## How to Participate
To participate in the bug bounty program, please follow these steps:
1. Identify a security vulnerability in our software
2. Report the vulnerability to us through our security contact email
3. Provide detailed information about the vulnerability, including steps to reproduce
4. Wait for our response and follow any instructions we provide

## Rules
* Only one reward per vulnerability
* No self-exploitation of vulnerabilities
* No exploitation of vulnerabilities for personal gain
* No disclosure of vulnerabilities to third parties
* No use of automated tools to scan for vulnerabilities

## Terms and Conditions
By participating in the bug bounty program, you agree to these terms and conditions.
